Beards and Industry take 2

Game flow:

Start with 7 dwarves, 6 assigned to hunting and gathering.  1.2 food per second per farmer, so net of 0.2 food

Basic storage is 1000 food, 1000 wood, 1000 stone, 1000 build points.

Buttons available are, "Gather food" "Gather Wood" "Gather Stone" "Construction buildings" "Build Hut"

At 8 huts, another dwarf arrives and the jobs button appears.

*****

Administrators generate policy points to enable some options.  As PP spending increases, so does PP spending, necessitating even more administrators!
